BMW E90 3 Series Sedan

The BMW E90 3 Series Sedan, launched in 2005, is the embodiment of the ultimate driving machine. With a focus on balancing performance with luxury, the E90 offers a premium driving experience. It features a sharp, aggressive design that signifies its sporting intentions, while the interior provides a blend of comfort, style, and functionality.

Features

  • Advanced Safety Systems: Including dynamic stability control and multiple airbags.
  • EfficientDynamics: Technology for improved fuel efficiency and lower emissions.
  • Luxury Interior: High-quality materials and innovative design.
  • Optional xDrive: BMW's all-wheel-drive system for enhanced traction.
