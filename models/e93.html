BMW E93 3 Series Convertible

Launched in 2007, the BMW E93 3 Series Convertible blends the sportiness and luxury of the 3 Series with the joy of open-top driving. It features a retractable hardtop that offers the best of both worlds: the security and quietness of a coupe with the exhilaration of a convertible. The E93 is perfect for those looking to add a touch of excitement to their driving experience.

Features

  • Retractable Hardtop: Smooth operation and excellent noise insulation.
  • Wind Deflector: Reduces turbulence when driving with the top down.
  • Heated Front Seats: For comfort during colder months.
  • Adaptive Headlights: Adjusts the light pattern based on driving conditions.
