Introduced in 2005, the BMW E91 3 Series Touring brings versatility to the 3 Series lineup, combining the performance and luxury of the E90 with increased cargo space. It's designed for those who need the practicality of a wagon without compromising on driving dynamics. The E91 is perfect for adventures, offering ample room for passengers and gear alike.

Features

  • Panoramic Sunroof: For an airy, open cabin feel.
  • Flexible Cargo Space: With a split-folding rear seat.
  • Roof Rails: For additional cargo capacity.
  • Rear Park Distance Control: For easier parking maneuvers.
